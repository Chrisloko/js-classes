Botox® Frequently Asked Questions

What is Botox?
It is a non-surgical aesthetic treatment made to relax musculature, extremely safe, and approved by the FDA to prevent and reduce fine lines/ wrinkles.
Is Botox bad for you?
No, absolutely not. We highly recommend the selection of a clinic that purchases its products only from an approved FDA wholesaler.
At what age can I start Botox treatment?
The correct age is when lines and wrinkles are beginning to be permanently visible during no facial expression. Youthful confidence awaits at the best MedSpa in Palm Beach.
After Botox treatment, am I going to look natural?
Yes, absolutely. However, we highly recommend the selection of a trained and experienced practitioner to achieve the best cosmetic outcome. Transform your appearance and reveal your best self.
What areas can I treat with BOTOX?
  • Frown Lines
  • Forehead Lines
  • Crows Feet
  • Smoker Lip Lines
  • Gummy Smile
  • Masseter- Bruxism
  • DAO
  • Mentalis
  • Eyebrow Lift
  • Hyperhidrosis- Armpit
Will I have more wrinkles if I stop using BOTOX?
No. The aging process will continue at the same point as when you were first treated. As the medication fades away, facial muscles regain mobility causing the formation of lines and wrinkles all over again. Unlock timeless beauty and smooth away wrinkles.
Does Botox have any possible side effects?
  • Redness
  • Swelling
  • Headache
  • Bruises
  • Eye droop
