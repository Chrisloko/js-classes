Causes of Protruding Ears

It may be unilateral or bilateral, here are the main causes:

  • Poorly developed Antihelix.
  • Large Concha (bowl of the ear)

Am | a Candidate for Incisionless Otoplasty?

Eighty percent (80%) of all patients with protruding ears are ideal candidates for Incisionless Otoplasty. However, the other twenty percent (20%) eligibility depends on the patient's unique anatomy.

What is the Recovery for Incision-less Otoplasty?

There is absolutely no downtime with Incisionless Otoplasty. However, it is recommended to avoid lifting weights, pool, or beach and wearing helmets for 5 days to have the best cosmetics outcomes.
